# ⭐ Debrief

## Most Useful / Positive Feedback

* Simplicity of tasks
* Breaking tasks into small increments

---

## Concerns

### Timer

* May cause anxiety
* Suggested:

  * Silent timer
  * Subtle notification instead of visible countdown
  * Find some way to help remind ADHD users that they need to complete the task

---

## Feature Suggestions (Explicitly Mentioned)

* Minimalistic design to avoid distractions (even timer -- hide it or show black screen and show time left every 30 seconds or so in order to not be distracted by the numbers ticking itself)
* Customizable reminders / notifications
* Visual checkmarks for task completion (helps ADHD know what they achieved -- "it works")
* Option for positive reinforcement (sounds or visuals)
* “Fresh start mode” (daily reset concept)

---

## Task Behavior Insight

* Emphasized focusing on **one task at a time**
* Noted that users may get distracted or abandon tasks

---

# ⭐ Workflow Fit

* Suggested:

  * Keeping tasks simple
  * Allowing user choice in reminders and notifications

---

# ⭐ Key Observation

* Timer (core feature) may introduce anxiety depending on implementation
